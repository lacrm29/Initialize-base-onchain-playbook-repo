
# Strategy

## Approach

Focus on consistent and natural interaction within the Base ecosystem.

## Consistency

Maintaining regular activity over time instead of short bursts of high volume transactions.

## Diversity

Interacting with multiple dApps and tokens to create a broader activity profile.

## Risk Management

Avoiding large transactions and focusing on smaller, controlled interactions.

## Efficiency

Using low-cost transactions to maintain long-term activity without high expenses.
